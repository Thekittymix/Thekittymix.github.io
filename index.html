About Me

Who I Am

Hi!♡ I'm a game engineer and AI specialist with a passion for creating interactive experiences that educate and entertain. With both a masters and bachelors degree in Game Design and Serious Games from UC Santa Cruz, I blend technical skill with creative problem-solving to build meaningful digital solutions.

What I Do

I create immersive games and AI-powered solutions that address real-world challenges with purpose and creativity. My work spans from educational VR experiences that bring ancient history to life to corporate AI tools that transform how companies manage talent. I thrive on making complex technical concepts accessible and engaging. Whether I'm building in Unity, Unreal Engine, or other tools, I craft digital experiences that connect with people on a deeper level. My passion is creating technology that matters – games and applications that promote mental health awareness, celebrate diversity and inclusion, explore the nuances of human relationships, and provide meditative spaces in our digital world. I believe technology at its best isn't just functional – it's meaningful, accessible, and transformative.

My Portfolio

Dolby Atmos Sandbox

An interactive demo built in Unreal Engine showcasing prototype headphone metadata technology. This project involved implementing the Dolby Atmos plugin, recreating a 7.1 surround sound setup, and coordinating with music creators to develop immersive audio content.

Birth From Fire

A Virtual Reality educational game that combines meditation, narrative, and hands-on learning about the ancient process of smelting in Timna. This project was playtested at the Egyptian Rosicrucian Museum in Santa Clara, bringing history to life through interactive technology.

Heart of Enya

A published 2D narrative-driven RPG built in Unity that blends strategic gameplay with rich storytelling. Released on Steam in 2020, Heart of Enya invites players to navigate complex relationships and emotional journeys through tactical combat and meaningful choices. The game explores themes of personal growth and interconnectedness within a visually distinctive world where your decisions shape not just the outcome, but the emotional landscape of the characters. This project represents my commitment to creating games that entertain while fostering empathy and reflection.
